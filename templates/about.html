Favourite Games
My favourite games of all time are: Final Fantasy X, Runescape, Elden Ring, Age Of Empires, mentioned in the next 4 boxes.
Final Fantasy X
Set in the fantastical world of Spira, it follows the journey of Tidus, a young athlete mysteriously transported to Spira from his world, and Yuna, a summoner on a quest to vanquish the malevolent entity known as Sin.
Runescape
Set in the medieval fantasy realm of Gielinor, a world divided into different kingdoms, regions, and cities, players can travel throughout Gielinor via a number of methods including on foot, magical spells, or charter ships.
Elden Ring
Set in the Lands Between, governed by the Ring and its demigod offspring, players assume the role of the Tarnished, called back after being exiled, on a quest to find all the Great Runes, restore the Elden Ring, and become the Elden Lord.
Age of Empires
Players are tasked with guiding a civilization from a small tribe to a dominant global empire across different historical periods, making strategic decisions in managing resources, technological development, and military campaigns against other civilizations.
